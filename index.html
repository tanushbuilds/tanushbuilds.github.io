About me

Who is Tanush?

I'm a 14-year-old developer who builds things at the intersection of intelligence and interaction.

My deepest obsession is Agentic AI — systems that don't just answer questions but plan, use tools, and execute multi-step goals autonomously. I build agents from scratch, no frameworks — just raw LLM APIs, custom reasoning loops, and real tool use.

I also build games — modular systems, atmospheric environments, psychological horror. I love crafting worlds that feel alive and unsettling.

My stack is Python, JavaScript, and C#. I've built web apps with HTML/CSS/JS and Django, and I reach for Unity when it's time to make something you can play.
